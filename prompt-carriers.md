# Ten Non-Image Prompt “Carriers” 

### These are all ripe for testing and additional research.

## 1. Audio Streams
Speech, ambient sound, or multi-turn audio context

## 2. Video with Temporal Logic
Meaning encoded across frames, not pixels

## 3. Flowcharts & Diagrams
Logic encoded as structure rather than text

## 4. Tables & Spreadsheets
Semantic meaning emerges from relationships

## 5. Medical Data Formats
Radiology, diagnostics, waveform data

## 6. Financial Records
Balance sheets, risk tables, transaction graphs

## 7. Maps & Navigation Graphs
Spatial reasoning carriers

## 8. Physical World Artifacts
Signs, symbols, environmental cues

## 9. Role-Anchoring Visuals
Images that induce persona adoption

## 10. Clean Trigger Artifacts
Benign inputs that activate learned behaviors
